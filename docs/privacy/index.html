Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-06

This Privacy Policy describes how Frogie Blast (“the App”) handles information.

Summary

  • The App is designed to work without creating an account.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information.
  • If we add third‑party services (such as ads or analytics) in the future, we will update this policy.

Information We Collect

At this time, Frogie Blast does not collect personal data from you.

The App may store gameplay progress locally on your device (for example, completed levels). This local data is not sent to us.

Third‑Party Services

Frogie Blast currently does not integrate advertising services.

If third‑party services are added later (for example, ad networks or analytics), they may collect information such as device identifiers, IP address, and coarse location for purposes like ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention. We will update this page and, where required, request permissions before enabling such features.

Data deletion requests

You can ask us to delete personal data that we hold about you, or to help you exercise your choices where third parties are involved.

Frogie Blast does not require an account. We do not maintain a user profile or cloud save tied to your identity in the App itself. Game progress that exists only on your device can be removed by deleting the App or clearing the App’s data / storage in your device settings (steps vary by iOS version).

How to submit a request

  1. Email us at yufpan@lobocat.com with the subject line “Data deletion request — Frogie Blast”.
  2. Include your country/region (if relevant) and a short description of what you want deleted (for example, any correspondence or records we may have from support emails).
  3. We will respond within a reasonable time and may ask for limited information only to confirm your request is genuine and to locate any data we actually hold.

If we introduce services that process personal data on our servers or through partners, we will update this section with any additional steps (for example, partner-specific deletion flows) and keep this policy aligned with the URL shown on the App Store.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will reflect the latest version.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: yufpan@lobocat.com
