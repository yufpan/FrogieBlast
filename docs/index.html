Ads / tracking

Frogie Blast currently does not show ads. If we add third‑party ads or analytics in the future, we will update our privacy policy.

I want my data deleted

See Data deletion requests in our Privacy Policy: uninstalling the App or clearing its storage removes on-device progress; you can also email us using the subject line “Data deletion request — Frogie Blast” for any personal data we may hold (for example, from support correspondence).
