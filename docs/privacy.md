# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-02

This Privacy Policy describes how **Frogie Blast** ("the App") handles information.

## Summary
- The App is designed to work without creating an account.
- We do **not** knowingly collect personal information.
- If we add third‑party services (such as ads or analytics) in the future, we will update this policy.

## Information We Collect
At this time, **Frogie Blast does not collect personal data** from you.

The App may store gameplay progress locally on your device (for example, completed levels). This local data is not sent to us.

## Third‑Party Services
Frogie Blast currently does **not** integrate advertising services.

If third‑party services are added later (for example, ad networks or analytics), they may collect information such as device identifiers, IP address, and coarse location for purposes like ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention. We will update this page and, where required, request permissions before enabling such features.

## Children’s Privacy
The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will reflect the latest version.
